Education

Schools Cricket

The IOMCA works with primary and secondary schools across the Island to introduce cricket to young people. Our Community Cricket Coach delivers high-quality sessions in schools, with a particular focus on supporting girls' cricket across all six secondary schools.

We use ICC Criiio resources and best practices for delivering cricket in schools — ideal for Key Stage 1 & 2 — keeping large groups active indoors and outdoors with skills and games designed for the classroom and sports hall alike.

Key Stage 1 & 2
Fun cricket activities and games for primary school children, developing coordination and teamwork.
Girls' Cricket
Dedicated girls' cricket sessions delivered across all six secondary schools on the Island.
Inter-School Competitions
School festivals and competitions giving young players their first taste of competitive cricket.
Coaching in Schools
A 3-hour workshop programme using ICC resources for anyone delivering cricket in schools.

Future Red Caps

Future Red Caps is the IOMCA's flagship junior development programme, designed to inspire the next generation of Manx cricketers who aspire to represent the Island at national level.

The programme provides structured coaching in batting, bowling, and fielding with high standards of behaviour in a safe, supportive learning environment. Sessions run for players from U11 upwards, with dedicated girls-only sessions available.

For more information contact: Sally Green, IOMCA Community Coach

Beginners

Cric Start

Cric Start is the IOMCA's programme for children aged 3–9, providing a fun and engaging introduction to cricket through three tailored formats. All sessions are delivered at clubs across the Island.

CricKids
Ages 3–6
A great first introduction to cricket focusing on athletic development, social skills and basic cricket movement. Fun, non-competitive sessions perfect for very young children taking their first steps in sport.
CricSoft
Ages 7–9
High-speed modified cricket emphasising participation and enjoyment. Every participant gets equal turns at batting, bowling and fielding — the perfect bridge into club cricket.
CricGirls
Ages 8–11
A dedicated girls-only programme providing a safe and welcoming introduction to cricket. Launched in 2024 to encourage more girls into the game from an early age.
Cric Start Delivery Clubs

Sessions are currently delivered at Crosby CC, Cronkbourne CC, Finch Hill CC and Ramsey CC. Contact your nearest club or the IOMCA to find out when sessions are running near you.

Summer Camps

The IOMCA runs cricket summer camps throughout July and August at Cronkbourne Cricket Club. Camps combine skill development with fun in a friendly, welcoming environment — a highlight of the summer for young cricketers across the Island.

Camps are available across multiple age groups and skill levels, with dedicated female-only sessions. Places are limited so early booking is recommended.
