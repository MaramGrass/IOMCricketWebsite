Get Involved in Cricket

Whether you want to play, coach, officiate or volunteer — there's a role for everyone in Manx cricket.

Home›Get Involved
Play

Start Playing

Whether you're a complete beginner or returning to the game after a break, the Isle of Man has a club and a format of cricket to suit you. All eight clubs on the Island welcome new members.

From hard ball cricket to soft ball formats, there are opportunities for players of every age and ability. The IOMCA also runs several participation programmes to help you get started.

Find a Club
Hard Ball Cricket
Traditional cricket at clubs across the Island for adults and juniors.
Soft Ball Cricket
A great introduction to the game for beginners of all ages.
Indoor Cricket
Year-round cricket at our indoor facility in Douglas.
Super Silver Series
Competitive cricket for older participants — never too late to play!

Schools Cricket

The IOMCA works with schools across the Island to introduce cricket to young people. From Kwik Cricket to inter-school competitions, we have programmes for every age group.

CricKids
Ages 5–8. Fun introductory cricket activities.
CricStart
Ages 8–11. Structured introduction to cricket skills.
CricSoft
Soft ball cricket for those moving into the game.
Competitions
