Our Organisation

Isle of Man Cricket Association

The Isle of Man Cricket Association (IOMCA) is the governing body for cricket on the Isle of Man. We are responsible for all aspects of the game on the Island, from grassroots participation to elite performance.

We are affiliated to the International Cricket Council (ICC) as an Associate Member and to Cricket Europe, enabling our national teams to compete in European and global competitions.

Our mission is to grow and develop cricket across the Island for players of all ages, backgrounds and abilities.

Our Mission
To develop, promote and grow the sport of cricket across the Isle of Man for the benefit of all.
Our Vision
A thriving cricket community where everyone can participate, progress and excel in the game they love.
Our Values
Integrity, inclusion, respect and excellence — the values that underpin everything we do.
