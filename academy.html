Player Development

IOMCA Academy

The IOMCA Academy provides an intensive development programme for the Island's most talented young cricketers. Players receive expert coaching, strength and conditioning, mental performance support and representative cricket experience.

  • ✓ Specialist batting, bowling and fielding coaching
  • ✓ Physical conditioning programme
  • ✓ Video analysis and performance review
  • ✓ Mental performance support
  • ✓ Representative match opportunities
🌱

Emerging Players Programme

The EPP is the entry level to the IOMCA's elite pathway, designed for talented players who show the potential to progress into the Academy and ultimately represent the Island at senior level.

  • ✓ Regular group training sessions
  • ✓ Specialist skills development
  • ✓ Individual performance plans
  • ✓ Access to IOMCA coaches
  • ✓ Pathway to Academy selection
