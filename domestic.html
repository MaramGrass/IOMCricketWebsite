Young Players

Junior Cricket

Junior cricket on the Isle of Man caters for young players from the age of 5 upwards. With multiple age-group competitions, training programmes and festivals, there are plenty of opportunities to play and develop.

All clubs offer junior sections and the IOMCA runs island-wide programmes including Kwik Cricket, U9, U11, U13, U15 and U17 competitions.
