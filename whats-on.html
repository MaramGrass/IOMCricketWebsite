Personal Coaching

One-to-One Coaching

Personalised coaching sessions available year-round — tailored specifically to your game and goals, whether you're a junior looking to develop or a senior player wanting to sharpen a specific skill.

Individual Sessions

One-to-one coaching with an IOMCA-qualified coach focusing on batting, bowling or fielding. Sessions can be booked at our Indoor Cricket Centre or at a suitable outdoor venue. Duration: 45–60 minutes. Available year-round.

Enquire & Book → iomca@iomca.im

Pair / Small Group Sessions

Cost-effective sessions for two or three players working together — great for school friends or club teammates. Coaching is personalised to each individual within the group. Duration: 60–90 minutes. Available year-round.

Enquire & Book → iomca@iomca.im
How to book: Email iomca@iomca.im or call the IOMCA to discuss availability, pricing and coach allocation. Sessions are subject to coach availability.
